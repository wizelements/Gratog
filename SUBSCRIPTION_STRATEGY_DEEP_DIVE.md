## PART 3: RETENTION & FLEXIBILITY FEATURES

### Feature #1: Smart Skips & Frequency Control

**Best Practice from Industry Leaders:**
- 72% of wellness subscribers appreciate skip capability
- Skip rate: 15-20% of months (not cancellation)
- Customers with skip option have 3.2x longer retention

**Implementation:**
```
Customer Portal Options:
☐ Skip next shipment (no charge, auto-recharge next month)
☐ Delay 1-4 weeks (rescheduled shipment)
☐ Change frequency (every 2 weeks → monthly)
☐ Pause subscription (for 2-3 months max)
☐ Swap product (Golden Glow → Floral Tide for this month)
```

### Feature #2: Loyalty Integration

**Best Practice:** Ancient Nutrition + Recharge integration saw 38% LTV increase

```
Proposal for Taste of Gratitude:
- Subscribers earn 2x reward points vs. one-off buyers
- 100 points = $10 off subscription order
- Birthday bonus: Extra points in subscriber's month
- Referral bonus: Get friend a $10 discount = 100 sub points
```

**Economics:**
- $5 referral cost per new subscriber
- Average customer LTV increase: $45/year
- Payback period: ~1 month

### Feature #3: Cancellation Survey & Winback

**Best Practice:** Recharge reports 15-25% recovery rate on attempted cancellations

```
Cancellation Flow:
1. Customer clicks cancel
2. "Before you go" survey (1 question):
   - "What caused the cancellation?"
   - Options: Price / Product Variety / Frequency / Other
3. Automatic offer based on response:
   - Price concern → "Try $14.99 Starter Sips instead"
   - Frequency → "Pause for 2 months instead of canceling"
   - Variety → "Swap your product for free this month"
4. If still proceeding: Email 20% off winback offer valid 30 days
```

## PART 7: RISK MITIGATION

### Risk #1: Low Quiz Conversion

**Risk:** Only 0.5% of visitors take quiz
**Mitigation:**
- Sticky quiz modal (exit-intent CTA)
- Simplified 3-question "quick version"
- SMS reminder to email non-converters
- Content marketing: "What wellness type are you?" blog posts
- Offer: Quiz = automatic 10% coupon (no subscription required)

### Risk #2: High Churn (>8%)

**Risk:** Customers cancel before 3 months
**Mitigation:**
- Skip/pause feature reduces perceived need to cancel
- Email engagement: Tips for using products, recipes, wellness education
- SMS: Mid-month "how's it going?" check-in
- Surprise delights: Random free shot in month 2
- Frequency optimization: Allow monthly → bi-weekly swaps

### Risk #3: Inventory/Fulfillment Complexity

**Risk:** Managing 5 bundles × 4-5 SKU variations per bundle
**Mitigation:**
- Start with 3 fixed bundles (less variation)
- Use smart rotation logic: Prioritize low-stock products
- Batch fulfillment on specific days (e.g., all subscriptions ship Mondays)
- Clear inventory forecasting: Monthly subscription = 30-unit minimum per SKU

### Risk #4: Seasonal Demand Fluctuation

**Risk:** Immunity Guard drops 60% in July-August
**Mitigation:**
- Allow free product swaps during off-season
- Create seasonal limited editions (e.g., "Summer Boost" bundle)
- Discount annual prepay to balance monthly variability
- Cross-sell: Summer offers → Fall immunity prep marketing
