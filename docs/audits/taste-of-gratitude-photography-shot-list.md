# Taste of Gratitude — Photography Shot List

This list is derived from the conversion-psychology audit. It prioritizes the images that will most improve trust, product understanding, and conversion.

## Priority legend

- **P0:** Blocks launch or currently damages trust.
- **P1:** High conversion impact; should be scheduled within 1–2 weeks.
- **P2:** Supports brand depth and repeat purchase.
- **P3:** Nice to have for future campaigns.

## Shots to avoid

1. Generic yoga, spa, or smoothie-bowl wellness stock imagery.
2. Pool-grown or obviously artificial sea moss.
3. Third-party brand labels visible in frame.
4. Lids on bottles when someone is shown drinking.
5. Glass juice bottles if current products use plastic bottles.
6. Generated or AI images passed off as photography.

## Technical requirements

- **Aspect ratios:** 4:3 (cards), 1:1 (product detail), 16:9 (heroes), 9:16 optional for social/Reels.
- **Resolution:** minimum 1200px on short edge for web; 2400px for zoom/print.
- **Color profile:** sRGB.
- **File format:** WebP with JPEG fallback; original RAW/HEIC retained.
- **Alt text:** Descriptive, including product name and format.
- **Naming convention:** `tog-{category}-{product}-{shot}-{orientation}-{YYYYMMDD}.{ext}`

Example: `tog-drink-kissed-by-gods-hero-portrait-20260722.webp`
